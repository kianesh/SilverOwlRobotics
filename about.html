Co-Founder

I'm the cofounder of Silver Owl Robotics and a member of 3150A. while a participant of the team I was a 2 time provincial champion and held programming lessons for many of our new or inexperienced members. I head our mathematics and problem solving lessons aswell as coach for our VRC teams.

Li Shi

Founder

As one of the team administrators and coordinators of Silver Owl Robotics, my role is to manage the club as well as the team. This includes mentorship, teaching, and inspiration to our members. As a member of 3150A, my job is to design and build robots. I also help with programming, especially with writing the routes, for match auto, and programming skills. Furthermore, I also draft strategies for the match. In my free time, not that I have any, I occasionally do mountain biking.

I plan on staying as part of the team for the next season to help run the business to provide robotics and mathematics education.
